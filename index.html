About ASKS


We are a group of women and femmes involved in the New York kink and sex scene.

Navigating through new spaces and communities can be exciting and fun, but it can also present challenges and uncertainties. Important questions and valid concerns are common and finding a place to bring them may feel intimidating. But that doesn’t mean they should go unaddressed.

We act as a safe support system for each other and wish to do the same for others in our community.

We are lifestyle and professional practitioners of kink. Most of us are very active in the New York kink community -- though you don’t have to be! -- and intimately know how hard it is to find safe spaces and set safe boundaries. Many of us have unfortunately been victims of relationship abuse, sexual violence or consent violations, and have learned to heal and find power in the scene. Additionally, some of our members have participated in trainings to better support survivors of violence and trauma.

Services


While we are not trained mental health professionals, we can refer you to resources where necessary. Most of all, we are here to lend a supportive ear to concerns and questions you may have about the kink scene, practicing polyamory, and consent issues both in public and in private.
